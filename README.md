# E-Commerce Ad Performance Dashboard

This Power BI project analyzes e-commerce ad campaigns across multiple digital platforms to measure performance and ROI.  
The dashboard visualizes KPIs such as **Impressions, Clicks, Cost, Conversions, Revenue, CTR, CPC, and ROAS** to help optimize marketing spend.



## Objective
To evaluate ad performance across platforms (Google, Amazon, Instagram, Facebook, etc.) and identify which channels deliver the best return on investment.



##  Key Insights
- **Amazon** campaigns deliver the highest revenue and ROAS.
- **Instagram** drives high clicks but moderate conversions.
- **YouTube** underperforms compared to cost — needs creative optimization.
- **Overall ROI** and conversions improved from August to October.



##  Tools & Skills
**Power BI** – Dashboard creation, Data visualization  
**Excel** – Dataset preparation and KPI calculations (CTR, CPC, ROAS)  



## Visuals Included
- KPI cards (Impressions, Clicks, Cost, Conversions, Revenue)
- Revenue vs Cost trend by month
- ROAS by platform
- Ad spend distribution
- Conversions by month
- Interactive slicers (Platform, Month)
